Pork Tenderloin Diablo

Description

If you love pork tenderloin, you'll love this recipe! Mustard, horseraddish, with a dash cayenne and cream combine to make a unique zesty sauce that will taste so good it seems wrong. If you eat pork, this is a must try recipe. It's also extremly easy to prepare, so no matter your cooking expierence, you can whip up this dish in less than an hour!

Ingredients

  • 1 (1 pound) whole pork tenderloin
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon extra-hot prepared horseradish
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon cold butter
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh chives
