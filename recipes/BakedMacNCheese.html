Baked Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Macaroni and Cheese is a food that many people have fond memories of. For some, it is a homemade favorite that mom made, either just as a normal dinner, or for some sort of special occasion. For others, it might be their a meal that represents their indepence because it is a meal they learned how to cook on their on as a starving university student, which makes sensen because Baked Macroni and Cheese is a cheap, yet cheesey and delicious meal that anyone can make, and everyone can enjoy.

This recipe is so simple that anyone can put it together in about 10 minutes and then just throw it in the oven. Shortly after that, you'll be able to enjoy a delicious creation of cheesey goodness and fond memories. Check the list below for a list of ingredients and directions to get started!
